# Material Plane
The material plane lays in the middle of realms refers to three parallel realities:
- The [[Waking World]] (also known as true world) is the realm in the middle of just about everything and is were most adventures take place.
- The [[Dream realm]] (also known as Feywild, World of colors and land of fey) is a more colorful and emotional almost extreme reflection of the waking world.
- The [[Sleeping realm]] (also known as Shadowfell, Land of darkness or World of Gray) is a more monotone, jagged and muted reflection of the waking world.

These three are by far the easiest realms to travel between with pathways existing in many places even if most are temporary and unstable. All these three realms occupy the same point in "space" and are "as real" as each other. 

Events in one of these worlds have a tendency to reflect in each other one given enough time making the worlds always to drift to being a similar state.
