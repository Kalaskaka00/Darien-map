# Dream Realm
The Dream realm is a part of the [[Material Plane]]. The Dream Realm is known to be colorful, insane and filled with spirits. 

The Dream realm is usually thought of as the opposite of the [[Sleeping realm]] and a reflection of the [[Waking World]]. The Dream realm is shaped by ones emotion meaning it can be booth a wonderful and colorful place as well as a horrific nightmare.

The Dream Realm is also known as the Feywild, World of colors and land of fey. The lands are mostly inhabited by fey creatures.
