# Divine Artifacts
It is said that each of the major gods and possibly even the minor Gods have left an artifact in this world. It is a well discussed question among theologians if this is a part of divinity, a part of human faith, long lost human artifacts or an item given from the Gods themselves.

It is well known that each major diety has one artifact, allthough some has bveen lost or has unknown locations, it is currently unknown if minor Gods posses similar artifacts.

The Major artifacts:

- Blood sickle of [[Bann]], a weapon who feeds on blood and violence, said to drive its wielders to sheding blood. Its locations is unkown.
- [[Dunden]]s Divine Blade, a weapon with powers of storms and dueling. Currently possed by [[Mivon]].
- [[Erven]]s Hammer of creation, part tool part warhammer it is rummroed to create grat items of renown. Its is thought to be possesed by some dwarf hold.
- [[Rendeen]]s Bow of the Huntress, a powerful bow said to never miss its target. Rummored to be posessed by some tribe in [[Embeth]].
- [[Sandor]]s great trident, a trident said to grants its wearer many abilities linked to water and oceans. Currently possesed by [[Numeria]].
- [[Thirdenlin]]s Staff of power, a staff said to amplify magical abilities and grant knowledge. It is posessed by [[Pitax]].
- [[Vanee]]s gift, an amulet said to grant life. It is said to be possesed by [[Touvette]] and possibly even be part of their great agricultural wealth.
- [[Xiander]]s eye, a crystal ball said to grant power of death and destiny, rummors goes it drives any wielder mad. Its location is unkown.
