# Divine Artifacts
It is said that each of the major gods and possibly even the minor Gods have left an artifact in this world. It is a well discussed question among theologians if this is a part of divinity, a part of human faith, long lost human artifacts or an item given from the Gods themselves.

It is well known that each major deity has one artifact, although some has been lost or have unknown locations, it is currently unknown if minor Gods posses similar artifacts.

The Major artifacts:

- Blood sickle of [[Bann]], a weapon who feeds on blood and violence, said to drive its wielders to shedding blood. Its locations is unknown.
- [[Dunden]]s Divine Blade, a weapon with powers of storms and dueling. Currently possessed by [[Mivon]].
- [[Erven]]s Hammer of creation, part tool part warhammer it is rumored to create great items of renown. Its is thought to be possessed by some dwarf hold.
- [[Rendeen]]s Bow of the Huntress, a powerful bow said to never miss its target. Rumored to be posessed by some tribe in [[Embeth]].
- [[Sandor]]s great trident, a trident said to grants its wearer many abilities linked to water and oceans. Currently possessed by [[Numeria]].
- [[Thirdenlin]]s Staff of power, a staff said to amplify magical abilities and grant knowledge. It is possessed by [[Pitax]].
- [[Vanee]]s gift, an amulet said to grant life. It is said to be possessed by [[Touvette]] and possibly even be part of their great agricultural wealth.
- [[Xiander]]s eye, a crystal ball said to grant power of death and destiny, rumors goes it drives any wielder mad. Its location is unknown.
