# Magrie Beeret
*I can´t be bothered with all these titles and formality.*

Magrie is the third child of [[Keldithas Beeret]] and is rumored to be illegitimate. She is said to be more interested in music and arts than politics. She was rumored say she has a tendency to sneak out from the mansions and perform in taverns.

Magrie became an envoy of [[Brevoy]] sent to the [[Golden Lands]] where she became a councilor. She became famous for her happy demeanor and informal methods.

## Relations
- [[Keldithas Beeret]] is Magries father.
- [[Shaharice Boldshard "Chirp"|Chirp]] and [[Reina Palett]] are close creative friends.
