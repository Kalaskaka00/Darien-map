# Bille Frostbite
*Might and magic!*

Bille grew up in [[Pitax]] during [[The Rill War]] war where he was quickly learned to use magic. Bille would eventually leave this behind and start traveling as a mercenary for hire.

Bille was one of the [[Stag Lord]]s lieutenants known as a great and powerful mage with a wicked temper.

Since he was defeated by [[Silvana Ashford]], [[Bree Caley]] and [[Claire Nightshield]] and they captured him and hired him to defeat the [[Stag Lord]]. After that he was appointed to the [[Golden Lands]] council.

Bille has taken [[Annj Bearer]] under his wing but he does insist that he doesn´t have much to teach her, but is rather just an enthusiastic spectator.

## Relations
- [[Kesten Garess]] is a close friend and ally of Billes.
- [[Annj Bearer]] is someone whom Bille has mentored and gave her her first spell book
- Bille used to be one of the [[Stag Lord]]s lieutenants.
- While serving in the [[Stolen Lands]] Bille worked with [[Ox]], [[Xer]], [[Crash]] and [[Siphon]].
