# Siphon
*Wanna bet on how many grapes I can throw in to [[Crash]]es mouth before he wakes up?*

Sylthana originates from [[Pitax]]. Her father was a great mage and her mother a traveling musician. Her mother died soon after birth and her father kept her form public view.

At the age of 14 Sylthana fled from home with a deep distaste of nobility and class systems. She traveled around taking different odd jobbs, she eventually started traveling with Crash who helped her out.

She and [[Crash]] would eventually head to the [[Stolen Lands]] and aid the [[Stag Lord]]. It was however during this job when she was scouted by [[Silvana Ashford]], [[Bree Caley]] and [[Claire Nightshield]] who recruited Siphon to their side.

## Relations
- [[Crash]] is like an older brother to Siphon and has kept her safe for long.
- [[Zara]] is a fellow free spirit whom Siphon likes to spend time with.
