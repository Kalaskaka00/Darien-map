# Annj Bearer
*That´s fascinating!*

Annj is the daughter of [[Movh Bearer]] and lives with [[Adsatra Sagebrook]]. She hails from Numeria but they would venture into the [[Summerborn]] to establish a new tavern and life.

She has an owl familiar named Tea.

Due to mentorship of [[Bille Frostbite]] and resources and trust from [[Silvana Ashford]], [[Bree Caley]] and [[Claire Nightshield]] Annj studied magic and vowed to become a mage. 

She would later go to [[Candelmore]] to study with the [[Candle Mages]] and sharpen her skills further.

## Relations
- [[Movh Bearer]] is Annjs father, Annj mother died in childbirth.
- [[Adsatra Sagebrook]] took care of Annj and [[Movh Bearer]] when Annj grew up.
- [[Bille Frostbite]] has been a great mentor of Annj, he gav her her first spellbook and gave her confidence to advance in life.
