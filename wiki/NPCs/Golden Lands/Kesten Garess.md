# Kesten Garess
*It shall be done my lady.*

Kesten is the guard captain of [[Aldori family]] household. He is a distinguished warrior who has been in a few battles during his time including fighting in [[The Rill War]] and [[Battle of Fingers]].

After helping [[Silvana Ashford]], [[Bree Caley]] and [[Claire Nightshield]] defeat the [[Stag Lord]] Kesten became a trusted advisor of the [[Golden Lands]]. 

Kesten lost an eye and was severely wounded when fighting a few trolls.

## Relations
- Kesten serves under [[Silvana Ashford]], [[Bree Caley]] and [[Claire Nightshield]].
- [[Bille Frostbite]] is a close friend and ally of Kesten.
- Kesten has [[Crash]] and [[Siphon]] under his command.
- Kesten used to serve under [[Jamandi Aldori]].
