# Iandri Flukeflow
*Hmmmmmm, what did you say?*

Iandri Flukeflow is a court mage for the [[Aldori family]] having served the kingdom of [[Brevoy]] for many years. He retired as a grand mage and battle wizard during [[The Rill War]], as he said age had made him to wise to battle.

Since then he mostly researches on his own and occasionally lends his services to the [[Aldori family]] and other people.

## Relations
- [[Jelbli Flukeflow]] is my neice.
- Iandri is an advisor to [[Jamandi Aldori]]
