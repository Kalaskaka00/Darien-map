# Milo Grainseller
*I can tell you're a discerning customers, how about I give you half price and save us the bartering?*

Milo is a merchant that comes from the long time mercantile [[Grainseller family]] whom are known for their business sense. Milo is the fifth child in the family.

Milo himself is a skilled merchant whom can run businesses and sense any hole in a market. Historically he mostly did business in [[Touvette]] along the [[Kings Road]]. 

Milo would eventually meet [[Bree Caley]] and became immediately infatuated, the two of them became romantically involved for a long time even if they only saw one another occasionally.  

As [[Bree Caley]] helped fund the [[Golden Lands]] she invited Milo and he immediately traveled to [[Summerborn]]. While there he became treasurer and made a fortune. However more importantly after a kidnapping event [[Bree Caley]] accepted his proposal.

## Relations
- [[Bree Caley]] is Milos fiance and the two of them has been engaged since {{year:3861}}.
- [[Vein Grainseller]] is milos mother and the head of the [[Grainseller family]].
- [[Malora Grainseller]] is Milos younger sister.
- [[Wit]] is Milos friendly rival.
