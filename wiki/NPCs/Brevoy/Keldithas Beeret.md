# Keldithas Beeret
*A little bird told me something that might interest you, I´d be happy to share for a price.*

Keldithas is the undisputed head of the [[Beeret family]] and is probably the richest man in [[Brevoy]] except for the king thesemlf. Except his wealth he is known for being intrested in the arcane and knowing about almost anything that happens in the Realms.

He has gotten the name "Silver Seer" as a reference to his wealth, entrepreneurship and knowledge of the happenings.

## Relations
- [[Magrie Beeret]] is Keldithas daughter.
- [[Sandak Viine]] is Keldithas greatest rival.
