# The Stag Lord
*I am the ruler of these lands, surrender now!*

Durie was once a son to a shaman in the [[Stolen lands]], his father the shaman was however a bad man who continually abused his son. Duries mother was never around, maybe a fey maybe dead.

Durie was constantly abused by his father until a nature spirit [[Myron]] lent him some of her power to stand up to his father. Durie took the power, killed his father and never returned it. Durie used these powers and his shaman abilities to slowly grow in strength and influence living of others.

The Stag Lord and his crew of bandits were at year 3861 the closet thing the [[Stolen Lands]] had to a ruler. He and his crew founded [[Summerborn|Stag Keep]] in old ruins, which would after his death transform into [[Summerborn]].

He was eventually killed by a delegation of [[Brevoy]] led by the to be barons of the [[Golden Lands]] [[Silvana Ashford]], [[Bree Caley]] and [[Claire Nightshield]].

## Relations
- [[Myron]] was once a friend of the Stag Lord but he eventually locked her in an amulett to use her powers for himself.
- [[Ox]] was The Stag lords right hand man.
- [[Bille Frostbite]], [[Xer]] and [[Crash]] were  lieutenants of the Stag Lord.
