# Swordlords of Brevoy
**Locations:** [[Brevoy]]
**Headquarters:** [[Restov]]
**Type:** Military

*The Swordlords are an organisation of warriors training since childhood to wield blades. They are considered some of the best fighters with swords. **

**Leadership:** The Swordlords are lead by a council of 3 swordlords known as the swordlords of [[Brevoy]] they take leadership and are the final examiners for graduating students. The organisation is practically controlled by the [[Aldori family]] giving it the unofficial name of the Aldori Swordlords.

**Organisation:** When someone reaches maturity after training and succeeds a base test gets named a defender.

After showing merit on the battlefield and having at least 10 years experience one may upon recommendation be granted the title of sword duke.

Lastly the top 3 in the organisation are named Swordlords of [[Brevoy]].
