# Viine family
**Locations:** [[Brevoy]]
**Charges:** [[Southhost]]
**Races:** Halflings

The Viine own the greatest agricultural land in [[Brevoy]] and therefrom produce a lot of great food and goods. They are except for their agriculture also known to be savvy politicians who gain power through marriages.

The Viine are known to be friendly and calm.

The Viine operate out of [[Southhost]].
