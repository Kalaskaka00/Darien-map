# Black Mantles
**Locations:** [[Darien]]
**Type:** Criminal, Political, Trade

*Black Mantles are a group of smugglers and slavers operating outside the law. Many of them and the group as a whole has an anarchic worldview seeing order and laws as obstacles and problems.*

*The group has been known to aid rebellions in different regions.*

**Leadership:** The Black Mantles are led by an "Exarch" a leader whom practically owns the group. The identity of the Exarch is a closley guarded secret, only a handfull of members know who the Exarch is.

**Organisation:** Anyone is allowed to join the Black Mantles if they prove themselves to be capable and share their worldview. The organisations customs if there are any are shrouded in mystery.
