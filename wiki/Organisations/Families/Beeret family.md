# Beeret family
**Locations:** [[Brevoy]]
**Charges:** [[Roughen Fort]]
**Races:** Elves

The Beerets are known as the richest family in [[Brevoy]], and the newest of the great families. They have a lot of money invested in many ventures, but are especially known for their banks.

The Beerets are known to be cunning and greedy.

The Bereets have a lot of influence across [[Brevoy]] but has their power base at [[Roughen Fort]].
