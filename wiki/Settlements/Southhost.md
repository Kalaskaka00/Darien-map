# Southhost
*The wealthy breadbasket of [[Brevoy]]*

Southhost is a wealthy city with a notable wealth and art. Southhost lay in fertile and is surrounded by beautiful fields.

Southhost is known as a friendly city with many good inns and public facilities like public baths and parks.
