# Pitax City
*Pitax City is the capital of [[Pitax]] and is often called the city of mages*

Pitax City is a city of magic filled with scholars, wizards and some traders. It lays in the middle of [[Pitax]] on the [[Kings Road]].

Pitax City is known for its magical academies and containing the great magic and knowledge. They are know to be very strict with visitors and keeping close track of them.

## Economy 
Pitax city makes a lot of its wealth on tolls on through passing trade as well as selling magical services and books. The city is mostly self sufficient in food.
