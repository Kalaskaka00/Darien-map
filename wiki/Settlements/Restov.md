# Restov
*A small fortress city to the east in Brevoy, home of the [[Aldori family]] and [[Swordlords of Brevoy]].*

Restov is mostly a large castle and fortifications that has developed a city overtime. The city lays to the east and protects [[Brevoy]] from [[The Wastes]].

Restov is most well known for their swordsmanship and military prowess, especially the [[Swordlords of Brevoy]].
## History 

## Economy 
Restov is not a very wealthy city, much of its economy is based on the crown paying for defense and spoils of war.

Restov has enough food to feed its population without imports, but just barley.
## Important NPCs 
- [[Jamandi Aldori]] is the ruler, she is seen as rough but fair. She is respected as able to defend the city.
- [[Kassil Aldori]] is the second in command in Restov, people has little opinion of the man.
- [[Iandri Flukeflow]] is a mage and advisor in the city.
## Organisations/Groups
- The [[Aldori family]] rules the city and maintains an iron discipline.
- The [[Swordlords of Brevoy]] are the heros of the city and what every kid aspires to be.
## Notable Locations 
- The academy of blades. The academy of blades have trained some of the best swordsmen in the world and is a mandatory stop for all in the [[Aldori family]] and the [[Swordlords of Brevoy]].
