# Starfall
*Starfall is the vivid capital of [[Numeria]]*

Starfall is a city surrounded by mountains and farmlands with a large lake in the center. The city is said to lie on top of fallen stars which grant the rich soil for farming and mining.

Starfall is known for its beautiful landscapes and vivid mountains.

## Economy 
Starfall is quite self reliant when it comes to food and economy. They do however also have a mining industry that mines up a magical material called [[Stardust]] which they sell to mages, alchemists and astrologers.
