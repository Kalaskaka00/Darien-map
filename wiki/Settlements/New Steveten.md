# New Steveten
*The royal and noble capital of [[Brevoy]]*

Steveten is a precise city filled with knights, traders and adventurers. The city lays at the end of [[Kings Road]] bordering the beatiful [[Crystal Lake]].

New Steveten is known for its variety featuring people of many different lifestyles as well as its royal styles.
## History 

## Economy 
New Steveten has enough food to feed its citizens but also imports food from [[Southhost]]. The city is the center of [[Brevoy]] and therefore has access to many differing resources from the kingdom that it trades with merchants on the [[Kings Road]].
## Important NPCs 
- [[Talia Andureen]] is queen of [[Brevoy]] and rules the city. She is known to be quite pompus and care a lot about how the city looks and feels.
- [[Rita Neat]] is a close and vital advisor.
