# Roughen Fort 
*A small but important fort on the border between [[Brevoy]] and [[Numeria]].*

Roughen Fort is quite a small settlement with a large military presence and a steady flow of merchants. The fort lays in less fertile lands that does however have some natural resources.

Roughen Fort is more known for its memorial and banking than anything else.
## History 
Roughen fort was founded quite recently during [[the Rill war]] from newly conquered [[Numeria]]n lands.

For their efforts during the war ruler-ship of the city was given to the [[Beeret family]].
## Economy 
Due to its large influx om merchants and presence to many nations as well as the rich [[Beeret family]] it has become a land of banks.

Except for the banking and general trade Roughen fort has a small farming and lumber industry.
