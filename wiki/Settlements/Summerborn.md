# Summerborn
*Summerborn is the bustling city in the [[Golden Lands]]*, deep within the [[Stolen Lands]]

Summerborn is quite a small fortified city known for drawing unique and talented individuals.

Summerborn is known for its adventurers spirit and dangerous regions.
## History 
Summerborn was founded when the three adventurers [[Claire Nightshield]], [[Silvana Ashford]] and [[Bree Caley]] defeated the [[Stag Lord]] and took over his keep at the point called Stag Keep. They renamed the settlement and made it in to the home of the [[Golden Lands]].
