# Lonelywood
*Lonelywood is one of the Northernmost outposts of [[Brevoy]] constantly contending with snow and ice.*

Lonelywood is a coastal city that is inaccessible a large part of the year living on the minerals and ocean. Its home to a hardy people who believes on self reliance.

Lonelywood if known for its whaling industry and its hardy but kind people.
## History 

## Economy 
Lonelywood survives mostly from the fishing, whaling and supplemental hunting. It however makes money from its extensive mining industry.
## Important NPCs 
- [[Thorin Udoon]] is in charge of the city he is known as a honest and kind but not so bright man.
- [[Maameo Udoon]] is in charge of the citys garrison and security, she is renowned as a fearsome warrior.
- [[Kiri Udoon]] is the bright mind that has helped revolutionize the mining industry in Lonelywood. 
- [[Ekon Udoon]] is an adventurer who makes his home in Lonelywood but is not often seen.
## Organisations/Groups
- The [[Udoon family]] is the most prominent force of the town.
## Notable Locations 
- The Plummeting mines are the most known part of the city generating much of its income but are also well known to be scary and very cold even threatining creatures hardened agaisnt the cold.
