# Avendale
*The bountiful bustling capital of [[Touvette]]*

Avendale is a relatively small city with a grand food market surrounded by fields and fields of crops. The city is not very dense filled with notable gardens.

Avendale is especially known for its culinary talents and music.

## Economy 
Avendale uses its great amount of farm land and culinary talents to trade for great wealth. Even at times their master chefs traveling across the continent to serve at wedding, receptions and noble events.
