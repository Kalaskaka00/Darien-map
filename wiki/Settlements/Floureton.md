# Floureton
*Floureton is the city of art and architecture, located at the edge of [[Gralton]]*

Floureton is a city filled with artists, architects and artisans. It is filled with new ideas and is mostly run by the artist guilds.

Floureton is known for being a beautiful city with amazing architecture, its art is highly prized.

## Economy 
Floureton makes its money by trading with merchants along the [[Kings Road]] selling its impressive art and handiwork at a high prize. It also makes a good amount of money through tourism with many richer people visiting.
