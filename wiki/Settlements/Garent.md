# Garent
*The city of Guilds and trade, the capital of [[Gralton]]*

Garent is a huge city filled with guilds and markets also containing a giant harbor. It sits at the start of the [[Kings Road]].

Garent is known for its guilds and their intense intrigue as well as being a merchant city.

## Economy 
Garent has a decent food production but mainly makes money due to its position as possibly the greatest harbor in Darien directly on the [[Kings Road]] granting it an intense throughput of trade and ships.
