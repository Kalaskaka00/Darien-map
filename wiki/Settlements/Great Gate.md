# Great Gate
*The great Dwarven fortress in [[Boggy Ridge]] separating Numeria and Pitax.*

Great Gate is a massive wall, fortress and underground city going across the mountain pass in [[Boggy Ridge]]. The above ground city is little more than a fortress with the [[Kings Road]] going through it, but the underground city is a sprawling network of mines.

Great Gate is one of the [[Dwarven Keeps]] known for its terrifying depths and incredible mineral wealth and geothermal resources.

## Economy 
Great Gate uses its minerals and geothermal vents to power massive forges and refines a large amount of metall and smithing. It uses this to trade with passerby merchants for food and other nevessities.
