# Gralton
**Capital:** [[Garent]]

**Common Races:** Half-Elf, Halfling, Human
**Uncommon Races:** Aarakocra, Dragonborn, Gnome, Sea-Elf, Tortle, Triton

*Gralton is often described as the harbor of the [[Darien]] region and is the entry and exit of international trade through the [[Kings Road]] and therefor wields significant wealth and influence. Gralton can be thought of as a nation of guilds.*
## Terrain & Geography
To the north is Graltons only land border where it borders [[Touvette]] and [[Embeth]]. To the east is the inland seas with [[Galt]] on the opposite end. To the south outside the coast are [[The Isles]]. To the west across a small inland sea rests [[Daggermark]].

Gralton has a warmer temperateclimate with the coasts ensuring mild winters and summers. The lands themselves are mostly plains and farmlands with soem hills scattered throughout. Most people live at the coasts or the large rives passing through Gralton.
## Economy
**Resources:** Fish, Grain, Olives, Linen, Artisan goods, Artistic goods, Naval supplies, Books
**Technology:** Renaissance, Skilled artisans with materials like glass, leather, cloth and other "artistic" materials
## Politics
Gralton is ruled by a council of the 8 most powerful guilds in the nation, where each guilds manages a part of Gralton politically.

The cities in Gralton and many guilds have significant autonomy and often drive different types of monopolies. Gralton in practice doesn´t collect taxes but rather relies on the guilds for funding, and the guild use their influence to gain advantageous treatments.

The most well known guilds of Gralton are:
-The [[Brass Wheels]] whom operate the trade and the [[Kings Road]].
## Culture
Gralton is a nation defined by three things: guilds, meritocracy and capitalism.

Almost everyone in Gralton is associated with one guild, their association forms a lot of their life style. People who are not part of a guild are called "guild less" and have a lower status in society. 

Gralton is very meritocratic with skill ruling supreme. One is supposed to find what they are good at and hone those skills. Promotions based on bribes or relation are seen as morally repugnant.

In Gralton every person has a purpose to act thrifty and make their own lot in life. Passiveness is the enemy of progress, or so is a common saying.

Gralton has no noble classes or other high society, the status in Gralton is more determined of your position in a guild and wealth.
## Faith
**Notable Deities:** [[Sandor]], [[Vanee]]
**Disliked Deities:** [[Dunden]], [[Xiander]]
## Relations
**[[The Isles]]:** [[The Isles]] are close allies with Gralton. [[The Isles]] help protect Gralton trade routes and Gralton help keep the Islands supplied.

**[[Touvette]]:** [[Touvette]] and Gralton hold friendly relations, although it has at many times been called for historically for Gralton to integrate Touvette into their nation. Such discussion are however fundamentally futile as long as the [[Manchu Tribes]] guarantee [[Touvette]]s security.

**[[Manchu Tribes]]:** The [[Manchu Tribes]] are viewed with great distrust. There is often a fear that the [[Manchu Tribes]] may invade Gralton if given the chance.

**[[Galt]]:** [[Galt]] are strategic adversaries of Gralton. The nation represent everything which people in Gralton see as wrong in culture and politics. Furthermore there is a general fear that their fledgling navy might come to challenge Graltons one day.
## History
**Founding History:** Gralton was once many independent cities vying for power and influence and fighting with one another. It was however guilds that started operating throughout the cities that slowly unified the cities and created commonality.
