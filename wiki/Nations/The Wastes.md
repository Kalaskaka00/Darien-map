# The Wastes
**Capital:** -

**Common Races:** Goblins, Hobgoblins, Orcs
**Uncommon Races:** Bugbears, Half-Orcs, Humans, Kobolds, Tieflings

*The wastes describes a large emptiness without any real state. This land is mostly empty deserts with roaming bands of Goblins, Hobgoblins and Orcs. There is however the possibility of fabulous wealth for those who can make the trip through meaning there are some signs of civilisation and some travelers and traders.*
## Terrain & Geography
The Wastes are a large area nestled between some mountain ranges and are almsot empty of features except for them. 

The area is mostly desert with some oasis and some areas with some savannas and shrubblands. The mountains and their complex cave systems also provide home to many creatures.

## Politics
There's the [[Greenskin]] tribes who have some civilisation.... and the kobold clans.... except for this there are only small outposts.

In the [[Greenskin]] tribes strength is king, the leader is almost always the most powerful in the tribe (usually an orc). They spend much of their time raiding, training and fighting. They should however not be underestimated, they have through their folk tales knowledge of practically every source of water and food in the entire desert. Though this they are among few who can navigate the area without serious risks. 

As for the kobold Clans they mostly live inside the caves and generally live under a strict priesthood worshiping dragons, depictions of dragons or dragon like creatures.
## Culture
Among the [[Greenskin]]s the Orcs are seen as the greatest warriors and are usually the defacto leaders of the tribes, they are usually violent and desperate to prove their mettle. The Hobgoblins are usually the intelligent tribal members acting as quartermasters, shamans and oversee the goblins. The goblins are generally at bottom of the hierarchy acting as workers and servants.

The Kobold clans live mostly isolated and have little contact with the outside world. They worship dragon kind and are cowardly. There are however some circumstances where the kobolds have established relations with outposts and trade with them.

## History
**Ancient History:** There are some ruins that can be found in the Wastes that might indicate that some kind of civilization might once have existed here. But no one knows for sure.

**Recent History:** 
- The Wastes have become a dangerous but slightly lucrative trade route for merchants. This has led to some outposts towards the outskrits popping up, most notably the Oases.
- There has also been multiple expeditions in the recent years from [[Brevoy]] to hunt down enemy tribes.
