# Numeria
**Capital:** [[Starfall]]

**Common Races:** Dwarves, Goliaths
**Uncommon Races:** Aaracokras, Duregar, Firbolgs, Humans, Lizardfolk, Locathah, Owlin, Tritons

*Numera describes a huge area vaguely unified. They're known for their harsh climate and exotic furs.*
## Terrain & Geography
Numeria is bordered to the north by a large open sea that's frozen much of the year. To the west is vast wilderness. To the south Numeria borders som large rivers, swamps, mountains and beyond those the [[Manchu Tribes]]. Lastly to the east Numeria borders [[Brevoy]].

Numeria has a harsh and cold climate with temperatures in much of the area rarely being above freezing. Inside the vast expanses of Numeria are dense and deep forests, large mountains and lakes and a lot of hills.
## Economy
**Resources:** Goats, Wood, Adamantine, Gold, Furs, Fish, Whales
**Technology:** Tribal in the desolate parts slightly more Medieval in some of the larger settlements, Skilled woodworking
## Politics
The capital of [[Starfall]] has the Tsar which is a title passed down by blood. The tsar is currently [[Gulrim Frostreach I]] a dwarf about 260 years old. The emperor is known to be quite wise and patient and is rumored to have plans reaching past decades.

Outside the capital region where much of the wealth in form of minerals is located there only exists smaller villages, trading posts, forts and nomadic tribes. Most of these villages and tribes are autonomous either only paying lip service to the emperor or not even realizing they're part of a greater nation.

Historically the Locathah and Triton have caused trouble in the area as they seldom like the ideas of being ruled by a dwarven monarch who can't even visit their villages.
## Culture
The vastness and isolation of villages and tribes mean that Numeria has more different cultures and traditions than could ever be accounted for. Below are huge generalizations as the variation is huge.

In the capital region there is a strong state hierarchy and age and wisdom is revered. As such many of the leaders are old dwarves who use their wisdom to lead their peoples. The reverance of age means naturally that many of the shorter lived races have far less influence and power. Travelers are generally greeted enthusiastically as visitors are not all to common in the frozen north.

In the deep lakes the locathah and tritons create villages. The triton and locathah live more peaceful lives and spend much of their time tending underwater livestock and building monuments and works of architecture. Exploration of the vast oceans and land itself is something highly respected and there is a tradition when coming of age that one is to go out for an expedition lasting at least one year and find something new.

Except this there also exists a great many tribes often consisting of humans, goliaths and or lizardfolk. These tribes are normally nomadic and travel great distances over their lives. cultures between the tribes vary notably, the goliath tribes are however often more warlike, lizardfolk are often more focused on living with the nature and human tribes are often reliant on trading.

Lastly notable are the wooden villages sprawling in tree houses that are created largely by Aaracokras and Owlin. These villages often live very secluded, that said they often travel surveying the land and delivering messages in service to the crown. here individual freedom is valued highly.

## Relations
**[[Dwarven Keeps]]:** The [[Dwarven Keeps]] in Numeria mostly respect the authority of the Numerian tsar. They don't consider themselves part of Numeria but do pay some taxes and head some of the laws put forth by the Numerian tsar.

**[[Manchu Tribes]]:** The network of tribal relations between Numeria and the [[Manchu Tribes]] are very complicated and varied. In general the nations have relatively warm relations due to their similarities with tribes some tribes even considering one another as cousins.

**[[Brevoy]]:** [[Brevoy]] and Numeria have mostly neutral relations with one another, although the relations occasionally turn sour when a Numerian tribe launches raids towards [[Brevoy]]. Due to Numerias control of the [[Kings Road]] they have a threat to close it of towards [[Brevoy]].

**[[Pitax]]:** [[Pitax]] and Numeria as a whole have relatively cold relations, however [[Pitax]] has warm relations with some of the tribes in Numeria who they aided in [[The Rill War]].
## History
**Founding History:** Numeria as it is today grew forth during [[The Manchu Conquests]]. The rapid expansion forced many different societies to band together in order to form a united front against the outside threat. Eventually they ended up with an emperor from the biggest and strongest city.

**Recent History:** 
- Some Numerian tribes fought with [[Pitax]] in [[The Rill War]] against [[Brevoy]] and [[Mivon]]. The war was costly for those tribes as they were decimated in the [[Battle of Fingers]].
