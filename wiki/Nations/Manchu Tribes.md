# Manchu Tribes
**Capital:** -

**Common Races:** Centaurs, Humans
**Uncommon Races:** Aaracokra, Half-Orcs, Hobgoblin, Orc

*The Manchu tribes live over large grasslands as nomadic tribes. They normally ride on horseback (or similar), living of the land and participating in raids and actively trade with neighbors.*
## Terrain & Geography
To the north as the climate becomes colder and more forested the influence of the Manchu tribes disapears to [[Numeria]]. To the east of the Manchu Tribes are some rivers and more fertile lands bordering to [[Pitax]] and [[Touvette]]. To the west and south the Mancu Tribes stretch far bordering  [[Daggermark]].
## Economy
**Resources:** Herbs, Wool, Horses, Livestock, Furs
**Technology:** Mostly tribal, Skilled working with hides, furs and animal parts, Composite bows
## Politics
There are many different Manchu Tribes each with a different Khan who rules the tribe. Chosen amongst all the tribes are the Khan of Khans who leads all the tribes. The current Khan of Khans is a Centaur about 30 years of age known as [[Wise Hoof]], he has made a name for himself by trading policies and forging alliances.

The Khan of Khans is elected for life and is elected by all other Khans at a tribal meeting. The Khans are usually elected both for military might but also their alliances. In practice the Khan of Khans tends to be the leader of one of the largest tribes.
## Culture
The Manchu tribes live of the land which they forever wander. The Manchu Tribes fight and trade with eachother, but will unite against any outside threat. Its normal during raids and trades to exchange/take women of the tribes with other tribes and less often men.

The Manchu tribes have strict rule where all men are expected to be warriors and leaders while all the women are expected to look after the livestock. The livestock too is vital for the life on the steppes, the livestock and horses are however seen as tools rather than companions.

The Manchu tribes value independence and freedom and have few laws and structures who decide whom to be.

The Manchu tribes have strong traditions and cultures connecting them to the lands. There are many locations which one is meant to visit over their life to complete ones upbringing. Similarly the land itself is worshiped with special care given to nature spirits.
## Faith
Most dieties are worshiped to more minor extents as nature spirits hold a more significant place in Machu religous life.
## Relations
**[[Touvette]]**: Manchu has warm relations with [[Touvette]]. There is a lot of trade between the two and the Khan of Khans has pledged to protect [[Touvette]] in the case of any aggression from its neighbors.

**[[Pitax]]:** The Manchu Tribes dislike the [[Pitax]]ians, they tend to treat the Manchu tribes as barbarians. The Manchus would probably raid [[Pitax]]ians regularly if it weren't for the strong defenses along the border.

**[[Numeria]]**: The network of tribal relations between [[Numeria]] and the Manchu Tribes are very complicated and varied. In general the nations have relatively warm relations due to their similarities with tribes some tribes even considering one another as cousins.
## History
**Ancient History:** The Manchus are said to have wandered in from the west and quickly dominated all open plains, being practically unbeatable in the fields. Since then their domain has shrunk notabley.

**Recent History:** 
-  During [[The Manchu Conquests]] the Manchu tribes and [[Numeria]]n tribes fought a lot with and against each other, internal squabbles almost led to the collapse of the Manchu Tribes.
