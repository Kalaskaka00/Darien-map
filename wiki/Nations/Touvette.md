# Touvette
**Capital:** [[Avendale]]

**Common Races:** Halflings, Harengons
**Uncommon Races:** Dwarves, Gnomes, Humans, Fairies, Firbolgs, Satyrs

*Touvetee has undoubtedly the best farmland in [[Darien]] littered with small villages. Touvette has used its farmland to assemble a notable wealth and exports a lot of its agricultural output to its neighbors. It has used its position to forge a neutral and peaceful stance in the world, they also therefore negotiate many disputes.*
## Terrain & Geography
Tovette has some large hills with [[Manchu tribes]] to the northwest. To the north is the land border to [[Pitax]]. To the east there's a river towards [[Embeth]]. To the south there's the rivers to Gralton.

Tovette is mostly dominated by farmlands and canals through the farmlands. Many claim that the farmlands are magically enhanced since ancient times producing a lot of high quality crops.
## Economy
**Resources:** Cotton, Maiz, Grain, Ale, Livestock, Cheese, Masonry, Music
**Technology:** Medieval, Some slightly more advanced technology when dealing with farming and food processing
## Politics
Touvetee has elections every three years where each village sends a delegate and the capital sends three delegates to the capital to form a parlament. The parlament choses amongst itself a first minister who leads the government. Touvetee does however not have political parties in questions and there is a lot of work to reach the most consensus possible. Each village also choses a mayor to lead the local village and the closest surroundings.

In Touvette there is practically no aristocracy similarly it is seen as the duty of the villages to make sure that all living there have a home and are fed.
## Culture
Touvette might be one of the most peacefull and plesent societies with a lot of music and parties. There are however some who for this reason finds it boring, there is generally very little wanderlust or curiosity amongst the inhabitants. Even in the politics people don't strongly argue they rather have a polite conversation.

The people living generally have little interest in the outside world. That said they do care a lot about taking care of their own. Similarly there is little use of coin in their societies outside the capital as one generally is assumed to give back something of equal value down the line.

People in Touvetee are generally warm towards outsiders, but suspicious of the outside world. 
## Faith
**Notable Deities:** [[Sandor]], [[Vanee]]
**Disliked Deities:** [[Bann]], [[Dunden]]
## Relations
**[[Manchu Tribes]]:** Touvette ha warmer relations with the [[Manchu tribes]] than most other civilizations. This comes to a large part from the notable trade conducted between Touvette and the [[Manchu Tribes]]. The [[Manchu Tribes]] have historically been a protector towards Touvette at times even threatening war towards anyone who's aggressive towards the nation.

**[[Pitax]]:** [[Pitax]] and Touvette have quite warm relations with the two sides trading a lot with one another. 

**[[Gralton]]:** Touvetee has neutral relations with [[Gralton]], although there have been at the very least talks in the past from [[Gralton]] that Touvette ought to be integrated.
## History
**Ancient History:** Touvette was settled long ago and might be one of the oldest living civilizations in the [[Darien]] region. The region is said to have been settled by halflings long ago who were aided by local Harengons.

It is said that [[Vanee]] herself blessed the earth of Touvette when she visited the place in human guise and was moved by the loving community.
