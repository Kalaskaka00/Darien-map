# Brevoy
**Capital:** [[New Steveten]]

**Common Races:** Halflings, Half-Orcs, Humans
**Uncommon Races:** Elves, Firbolgs, Goliaths, Half-Elves, Orcs

*Brevoy is one of the larger large nations in the [[Darien]] region and as such wields significant influence. They have a strong military tradition and have become known for their iron working and swords.*
## Terrain & Geography
Brevoy has a large coast to the north, but it is quite dangerous during the winter months to travel there. To the east is a mountain chain known as [[Icerime peak]] which is only passable through a few mountain passes. To the south are the wasteland known as the [[Stolen Lands]]. To the west are a few mountains and an open plain with a border to [[Numeria]].

Brevoy has a temperate climate with a lot of plains to the south, colder tundras to the north and a few forests spread around. The harsh mountains to the west and east are however practically uninhabited. The more southern regions are less densley populated as you get closer to the [[Stolen Lands]] and the sparse south east.
## Economy
**Resources:** Wood, Steel, Coal, Copper, Bronze, Fish, Mushrooms, Sheep, Mercenaries
**Technology:** Medieval, Skilled metal working, Rare use of gunpowder in mining
## Politics
Brevoy is ruled by a monarchy. The monarchy is currently head by queen [[Talia Andureen]], a human in her 60s. She is known to be a relatively weak ruler who cares a lot by prestige.

Behind the throne the true power are noble aristocratic families. Each family rules their local province, and due to the weak monarchy they have quite a lot of local power. The largest families are: The [[Aldori family]], The [[Beeret family]], The [[Viine family]] and The [[Udoon family]]. There are many additional noble families with varying amounts of influence and power.
## Culture
Due to the size of the nation there is a notable local variation in culture, especially between the northern tundras, the eastern forests and southern plains.

In general there is a strong sense of duty to your aristocratic leader, you are expected to respect those who rank above you. In return those with higher social status are strongly honorbound to protect and care for their subjects. This honor system runs through every level of society.

In the northern tundras in the harsher climate there is a string seafaring culture, with a tradition of fishing and shipbuilding. Additionally there are strong expectations to help strangers in need due to the harsh climate.

In the eastern forests people live in small villages with traditions of hunting and mining. This area has many great artisans and miners. Here there is a strong value of societal contribution, where one needs to contribute to society, and anyone who contributes less are looked down upon.

In the souther plains there is a strong farming culture and warrior culture. The large plains lend themselves to farming and the openess means raids are common. Here the warrior culture means a strong value is placed on fighting honarably and reaching significant strength.

## Relations
**[[Pitax]]:** Brevoy has a historical rivalry with [[Pitax]] with the two sides often undermining one another internationally, although rarely through pure war. Through this aim the two sides spend a lot of time spying on one another.

**[[Mivon]]:** Brevoy and [[Mivon]] are allies, the ally ship is mostly strategic but has also led to increased trade and relatively warm relations between the two nations.

**[[Numeria]]:** Brevoy and [[Numeria]] have mostly neutral relations with one another, although the relations occasionally turn sour when a [[Numeria]]n tribe launches raids towards Brevoy. Due to [[Numeria]]s control of the [[Kings Road]] they have a threat to close it of towards Brevoy.

**[[The Wastes]]:** Brevoy spends a great deal of time defending against raids from [[The Wastes]] and peridoacally launches its own expeditions into [[The Wastes]] to hunt down hostile tribes. Allthough this usually has little succees.
## History
**Founding History:** It is said that Brevoy originally consisted of 4 kingdoms each run by one of the great families but the kingdoms where eventually united under a king to face an outside threat. While its disputed what threat this was some claim it was [[Numeria]], some claim [[Pitax]] mentioning the ageless rivalry and some even claim that it might be along lost civilization from the [[Stolen Lands]].

**Recent History:** 
- The most defining event in recent history was [[The Rill War]] with [[Mivon]] against [[Numeria]] and [[Pitax]], this strengthened the [[Swordlords of Brevoy]] and defined modern relations for Brevoy.
