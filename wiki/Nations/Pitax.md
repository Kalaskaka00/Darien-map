# Pitax
**Capital:** [[Pitax City]]

**Common Races:** Elves, Humans, Tieflings
**Uncommon Races:** Changelings, Dragonborn, Genasi, Half-Elves, Gnomes

*Pitax is a city state originating from the powerful and renowned city of Pitax. Pitax is known for its arcane power, research and traditions, which dictate all of society.*
## Terrain & Geography
Pitax is bordered by mountains to its north. To its west is a river bordering the [[Manchu tribes]]. To the south nearer the farmlands is [[Touvette]]. Lastly to the east are the [[Nalmarches]] entering the [[Stolen Lands]].

Pitax is dominated by farming lands and plains across its border. Between the farming lands many rivers meander about in complex networks. In the north its slightly more wild terrain with forests and mountains. 

The climate is more moist and slightly colder in the north of Pitax but it has a year round warm climate in southern parts.
## Economy
**Resources:** Mithril, Arcane Magic, Cows, Food Crops, Wine, Paper
**Technology:** Medeival, More focus on advancing with magic than technology
## Politics
Pitax is ruled by the council of 9. The council of 9 is made up of a very skilled mage in each school of magic and the ninth the leader of council the Overseer is generally considered one of the strongest mages in the world, even though they're quite shrouded in mystery. 

With the focus on magic Pitax has some of the most prominent arcane academies in the world. Theese academies in turn have a lot of influence on the state and society at large.

Pitax politics are also known as quite dangerous with many layers of intrigues. As such assassinations are not uncommon.
## Culture
The theme of arcane magic permeates all of society where one can't hold a position of power (even something as guard captian) without being able to use atleast a cantrip. Indeed the society largley revolves around magic and its research.

There is generally a disdain in Pitax towards those who can't use magic and are of lesser races (ex: goblins, orcs). 

The large of influx of people who do trade in Pitax and come to study at the academies do however mean that the city of Pitax is a hub filled of exotic cultures and objects.

Slavery is legal in Pitax seeing as long as the slave can't show magical aptitude in which case the slave is freed and the state compensates the owner. Its these slaves who to a large extent work the farmland around Pitax generating food for the large city.
## Faith
**Notable Deities:** [[Thirdenlin]], [[Xiander]]
**Disliked Deities:** Everyone else has a far less pronounced role
## Relations
**[[Brevoy]]:** Pitax has a historical rivalry with [[Brevoy]] with the two sides often undermining one another internationally, although rarely through pure war. Through this aim the two sides spend a lot of time spying on one another.

**[[Touvette]]:** Pitax and [[Touvette]] have relatively warm relations with the two sides trading a lot with one another. 

**[[Numeria]]:** Pitax and [[Numeria]] as a whole have quite cold relations, however Pitax has warm relations with some of the tribes in [[Numeria]] who they aided in [[The Rill War]].

**[[Manchu Tribes]]:** Pitax looks on the [[Manchu tribes]] with disdain and consider them Barbarians.

**[[Daggermark]]:** Pitax is one of few nations who hold diplomatic relations with [[Daggermark]]. It is rumored that Pitax have at times loaned assassins and monsters from [[Daggermark]] and have in return offered magical services and expertise.
## History
**Founding History:** Pitax grew from the great [[Pitax City]] During the [[Devastation of Daggermark]] 900 years ago many of its mages flew to Pitax. This combined with the already existing magical universities have led to its magical prowess in the modern day as well as warmer relation with [[Daggermark]].

**Recent History:** 
- Pitax recently aided [[Numeria]] in [[The Rill War]] against [[Brevoy]] and [[Mivon]], this has defined Pitax relations in the latest decades.
