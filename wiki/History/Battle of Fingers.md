# Battle of Fingers
*[[@The Timeline]] Year {{year:3847}}*

Towards the end of [[The Rill War]] [[Roughen Fort]] was nearing its completion, the forces of the [[Numeria]]n tribes and [[Pitax]] made a last assault against the [[Brevoy]] and [[Mivon]] forces. The [[Brevoy]] and [[Mivon]] force was dug in but exhausted from previous battle.

The battle was incredible bloody taking many victims from all sides. [[Roughen Fort]] was almost overrun if it hadn´t been for [[Maameo Udoon]]s defense of the fort and the [[Swordlords of Brevoy]]s brave attack the battle would surely have been lost.

As the [[Numeria]]n and [[Pitax]] forces were repelled [[Roughen Fort]] was completed and peace was achieved.
