# The Timeline
Time in this world is defined with a year 0 at [[The Magical Anorchrism]] counting up since then. The timeline of major events can be seen below.

The years are sometimes put as AA for After Anorchrism or BA Before Anorchrism, but most common is writing the numbers in negatives and positives.

- **Year {{year:0}}:** [[The Magical Anorchrism]] where magic was unleashed from the gods to the people.
- **Year {{year:2923}}:** The [[War of the beards]] break out between the [[Dwarven Keeps]] and [[Daggermark]].
- **Year {{year:2972}}:** The [[War of the beards]] ends with the [[Devastation of Daggermark]] plunging the nation into despair.
- **Year {{year:3657}}:** [[The Manchu Conquests]] starts having the [[Manchu Tribes]] expand into [[Numeria]] rapidly.
- **Year {{year:3702}}:** [[The Manchu Conquests]] ends with the [[Manchu Tribes]] almost collapsing in on itself.
- **Year {{year:3843}}:** [[The Rill War]] between [[Brevoy]], [[Mivon]] and [[Numeria]], [[Pitax]] raged.
- **Year {{year:3847}}:** [[The Rill War]] ended in the [[Battle of Fingers]] and [[Brevoy]] building [[Roughen Fort]].
- **Year {{year:3862}}:** [[Kingmaker]] campaign starts.
