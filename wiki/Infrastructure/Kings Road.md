# King Road
The Kings Road spans from the major harbor city of [[Garent]] in [[Gralton]] all the way to [[New Steveten]] in [[Brevoy]], going along [[Gralton]], [[Touvette]], [[Pitax]], [[Numeria]] and [[Brevoy]]. The road acts as the central trade hub for all these kingdoms.

The Kings Road is arguebly the safest stretch of land on the continent. It is said that a halfling could walk naked with a bar of gold in their hands from one end to another without incident. The road is wide laid with stones, lit by lanterns and with many inns and resting places along the way.

The road was constructed long ago in ages forgotten by the dwarves laying a wide road of sturdy stones on the orders of the then Dwarven king. The settlement of [[Stone Road]] was founded in order to get sturdy ferrous stone which is a material that can stand just about any test of time. Some say it was built to further trade, others to move armies and show dwarven might others still said it was a gesture of love or friendship.

Since the fall of the Dwarven heartland the [[Brass Wheels]] trading company have taken over maintenece of the road to facilitate the traders intrests. The [[Brass Wheels]] also modernized the road with lightposts, signs, fences and mercenary guard patrols.
