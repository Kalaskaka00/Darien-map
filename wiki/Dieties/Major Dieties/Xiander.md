# Xiander

**Other Names:** The outsider, The wanderer, The great Diviner

**Aspects:** Death, Redemption/Rebirth, Destiny, Prophecies, Outcasts

**Associated Races:** Changelings, Drow, Half-Orcs, Shifters, Tieflings

**Domains:** Death, Grave, Order, Twilight

**Symbol:** An hourglass, three half circles inside each other each turned a qaurter, a droplette

**Description:** Xiander is often described as a wanderer, constantly shifting mirroring the people they meet, the only constant being that they have a gloss globe that fortells prophecies. Xiander has no agreed upon form as their gender and race is said to change to blend in with their surrondings.

Xiander is often thought of as watchfull, judgefull but forgiving. They have the dualty of both offerign starts and showing paths while simeltaniously being the one to end the paths.

Xiander is said to see against anyone who tries to avoid their destiny and anyone who makes a false redemption. 

**Worship:** Xiander is usually worshiped at graveyards at large stone altars at ground level. The altars often have depictions of a life story from birth til death.

Many of those who want to dedicate themselves to Xiander head out wandering searching for answers in the world. The clerics of Xiander are often grave keepers.

Burials to Xiander is often done by burying the body except for the heart which is burried under an altar to Xiander. When an altar is not available the heart is often burnt and the ashes spread. It is normal to put a rock above the grav to mark it.
## A story
There once was a small village with an elderly couple. The couple lived at the outscirts of the village shunned by the villagers for their apperance. 

One day a visiter with a strange face came during the evening and asked for bedrest cause they were tired, hungry and lacked any supplies needed to sleep outside in the cold. The couple said yes and showed great hospitality to the traveler, offering food and bedrest.

The travelers face was constantly hard to see, hidden in shadows and hard to focus on. They traveler eventually explained that every other house had turned them away because of their odd apperance and percieved untrustworthiness.

As the traveler left during the morning they said that in 4 days the couple should leave the village and go to an elderly oak two days away. There they will find a gift worthy of their kindness.

The couple left for the oak 4 days later and eventually made iot there. The couple found a small glass statuette that almost seemed to speak to them. Eventually they would both hear that they where hereby freed from their previous sins and were free to go on with their lives.

The couple perplexed walked home to their village found that all other villagers were dead, seemingly in a sudden desiease outbreak.The elderly couple worked hard burying all villagers and giving them worthy graves.

However as time apssed the couple grew stronger and younger. They couple would eventually come to have their own children and lead the renewal of the village.

The village is now said to never turn away those in need.
