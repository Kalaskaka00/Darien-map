# Vanee

**Other Names:** Earth Mother

**Aspects:** Farming, The Earth, The Sun, Tranquility, Fertility

**Associated Races:** Halflings, Harengons, Humans, Tortles

**Domains:** Life, Light, Nature, Peace

**Symbol:** Three wiggly lines ending in wheat stocks, A sun with radiating lines, a sunrise

**Description:** Vanee is often described as a farmer and mother, wearing a white dress and holding a golden staff ending in a radiant sun. Vanee is normally thought of as a she and described as a human.

Vanee is often thought of as loving, caring and peaceful. She has a duality in representing nature and the manm-made change of said nature to fields and farms.

Vanee is often thought of as against destruction and ruin. She sees poorly on people who do not share wealth and or show compassion to their fellow man. 

**Worship:** Normally Vanee is worshipped at a pillar stood so that it casts a long shadow during sunrise and sunset. The pillar is often without any special engraving and made simple. It is common to plant some flowers around the pillar.

Many dedicate themselves to Vanee by giving to charity and helping those in need. Amongst the clerics many give away most or all of their wealth and rely on the help of others to survive, theese clerics often refuse direct payment for any service they render.

The most common burials to Vanee is burying someone in a field during the sunset and then planting atop it. This is thought as returning the people to the earth.
