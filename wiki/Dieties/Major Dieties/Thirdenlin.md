# Thirdenlin

**Other Names:** Master of the Veil, The Knowing One

**Aspects:** Magic, Knowledge, Writing, Wits, Dragons

**Associated Races:** Elves, Gnomes, Dragonborn, Genasis, Kobolds

**Domains:** Arcana, Knowledge, Peace, Trickery

**Symbol:** A Dragons Claw, An open book with a quill laid atop it, A 7 sided "hexagon" (a sentagon).

**Description:** Thirdenling is often described as a schoolar, wielding a staff, wearing a long clokal and carrying some spell components and a quill. Rendeen is normally described as a male elf.

Thirdenling is often thought of as amoral, knowladgable and infinentley curious. He has a duality in both being knowledable and witty while simeltaniously lacking wisdom and compassion.

Thirdenling is against any "forbidden" knowledge and the meaningfull destruction of knowledge as well as holding back knowledge. He is thought of as agaisnt any limitation of thought and experimentation. He is usually thought of as against war and killing as it is a distraction from higher learning.

**Worship:** Thirdenlin is often worship at altars made in sntagonical shapes with arcane sigils. Theese altars are often surronded by bookcases.

A normal way to dedicate one self to Thirdenlin is to write some sort of work or recordning some sort of knowledge and adding it to the bookcases around his altars.

Thirdenlin does not have an exact burial ritual, the only real important thing is to record the deads name, years of death and living as well as a few lines about who they were at a special book inside a great altar.
