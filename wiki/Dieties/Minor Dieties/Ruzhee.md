# Ruzhee

**Other Names:** Mother of Spiders, Fate Ender, Fate Teller, Queen of Venoms

**Aspects:** Assasinations, Fate, Poisons, Scorpions, Spiders

**Assosciated Races:** Driders, Drows

**Domains:** Death, Grave, Nature, Trickery

**Symbol:** A circle with 8 lines petruding from it

**Description:** Ruzhee is often described as a ginormus drider sitting in an allacomensing web. She is from the web often described being able to see peoples futures.

Ruzhee is often thought of as cunning, a skilled manipulator and expert liar. Ruzhee is thought by some as a minor Godess that slowly influences people to steer the course of the world.

**Worship:** Ruzhee is often prayed to at special dedicated altars and she is generally only allowed to be prayed to directly by specific priestesses.

Ruzhee is a particular God most commonly beleived in by assasins, drow and driders. She is thought of as a dangerous ally and far more dangerous enemy.

Anyone dying to Ruzhee is aimed to be eaten by perferebly a priestess, secondhandedly spiders or scorpions.
