# Arie

**Other Names:** Grand Healer, The Great Pacifist, The Peace Maker

**Aspects:** Care, Healing, Life, Pacifism, Peace

**Assosciated Races:** Tortle

**Domains:** Life, Peace

**Symbol:** An open hand

**Description:** Arie is often depicted as an ancient tortle wandering equpied only with simple clothes and a wooden staff.

Arie is usually thought of as calm, wise and a great listener. She is often thought of as a wanderer who just wishes for people to live in peace with one another and to perserve life wherever possible.

**Worship:** Prayer to Arie is conducted without an altar by taking ones hand to their hearts, ears and eyes. This is meant to symbolise them feeling, hearing and seeing other people.

Arie rarley makes deals or any contact with other people.

Burial rituals with Arie usually consist of people being clothed simply and burried without a coffin.
