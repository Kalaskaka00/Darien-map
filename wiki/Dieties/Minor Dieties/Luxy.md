# Luxy

**Other Names:** Lady luck, dice godess, baroness of bets

**Aspects:** Luck, Gambling, Games, Betting

**Assosciated Races:** Halflings, Humans

**Domains:** Trickery

**Symbol:** A fiveleaf clover

**Description:** Luxy is often described as a halfling wielding a deck of cards in one hand and some dice in another. Luxy is mostly commonly shown as a female halfling.

Luxy is often thought of as excited, happy and relaxed. She is generally thought as being unworied by the world around her and giving luck to those willing to believe it.

**Worship:** Luxy doesn't have any form of organised altars or prayer, and her name is mostly invoked inside gambling dens.

When one wishes the luck from Dunden one often invokes her name and prays for her help. She is thought to somehow help though soem say that her giving luck at one point brings missfortune in the future.

There are no Luxy burial rites.
