# Ebus

**Other Names:** Master Architect, The Homesteeder

**Aspects:** Building, Homes, Peace, Self-Sufficency

**Assosciated Races:** Firbolg, Halfling, Harengon

**Domains:** Forge, Life, Order, Peace

**Symbol:** A house

**Description:** Ebus is often depicted as a firbolg working tirelessly to aid others, and guiding others in their work

Ebus is often described as patient, hard working and calm. They are often thought of someone who helps those who work hard but has little patience for people who don´t give it their all.

**Worship:** Ebus is often worshiped at construction sites or by architects, builders and people living on their own. Prayer is usually conducted during work on a project or symbolic work with some tools.

Ebus often works in the background and gives those people who work hard for themselves some slight help or inspiration from the background.

Burials for Ebus usually consist of people being burried in the ground with their most favored tools and the grave is usually topped with some construction materials, small structure or other handywork which is left to slowly decay.
