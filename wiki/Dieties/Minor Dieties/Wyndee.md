# Wyndee

**Other Names:** Curse maker, Hag mother, Old cackle

**Aspects:** Curses, Deformities, Hags, Vengance

**Assosciated Races:** Hags

**Domains:** Arcana, Nature, Twilight

**Symbol:** A decriped finger, a vodoo doll

**Description:** Wyndee is often described as a malformed hunchbacked hag wielding a bag of rotten herbs in one hand and a lantern emitting darkness in the other.

Wyndee is often thought of as cunning, wrathful and vengeful. She is thought of as someone who offers murky deals to gain vengance on ones foes. She is thought of as being dangerous to deal with as all her deals have terrible costs.

**Worship:** Luxy doesn't have any form of organised altars or prayer, she is often worshiped by doing sacrifices in her name.

When one wishes to make a deal with Wyndee one is tohught to have to get lost in the swamps or woods with vengance in ones heart. It's only then one might find Old Cackle.

Wyndee have no burials as such, the closest thing is sacrificing the corpse to her.
