# Xia

**Other Names:** The Lord of Stories, The great storyteller, The great seer

**Aspects:** Destiny, Prophecies, Stories

**Assosciated Races:** Changelings

**Domains:** Knowledge, Trickery

**Symbol:** Three quarter circles inside each other each turned a quarter, two lines with a star between them

**Description:** Xia is often described as a trickster or even melicious storyteller, seeing futures and trying to make certain stories unfold. Xia is often described as handsome young man or woman changing forms and adding a certain word at a certain point to change the fates.

Xia is often described as cunning, curious and heartless. They are often thought of as a charachter in the background subtly influencing events.

**Worship:** Xia has no form of worship.

Xia is often thought of as impossible to find, they find you if they wish to do so.

Xia´s onlu burial rite is that each persons life should be writen down as a short story or told as a short story and that story shall be burried with them.
